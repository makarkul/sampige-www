Who We Are

Pioneering India's Semiconductor Future

Sampige Semiconductors is a fabless semiconductor company founded by a team that previously built and exited India's first private fabless semiconductor company, Saankhya Labs — acquired by Tata Group's Tejas Networks for ₹500+ Crore.


Named after the Sampige flower — a symbol of growth and elegance native to Karnataka — we design silicon for consumer electronics, IoT infrastructure, and sovereign AI. Our founders bring 155+ combined years of experience in semiconductors, wireless systems, and AI-driven platforms.
